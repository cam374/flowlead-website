Your Questions, Answered

Quick, reassuring answers to the most common client questions.

Is there a waitlist when you're at capacity?

There can be. We focus on keeping quality high, so when our team is at capacity, we hold new requests on a waitlist until we can take on more.

What industries do you work with?

We work with all niches and industries as long as you have product-market-fit.

Do we ever go down because of email deliverability issues?

No, never. Our private infrastructure and rotating sending accounts ensure maximum deliverability and uptime, so your campaigns keep running smoothly without interruptions.

How long does it take to start seeing results?

Some clients see results almost instantly, but typically best results come between the 60-120 day mark and beyond.

What is your pricing structure?

We use a combination of retainer and performance models. Each approach is set up to give you steady coverage while still letting results drive the upside. The goal is to keep things consistent as you scale without boxing you into a one size fits all setup.

Is this a done-for-you service?

We can either handle the full journey through to a booked meeting in your calendar, or run the entire top funnel for you and pass new leads straight into your CRM for your team to take from there.
